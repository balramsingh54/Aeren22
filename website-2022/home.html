He has also been the Chief Justice of Karnataka High Court. He was also appointed as an additional judge of the Delhi High Court and on 30 October 2000, he was appointed as a permanent judge.

Hon. Justice P. P. Naolekar (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2004.

At Present, he is the Lokayukta of Madhya Pradesh.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Gauhati High Court. He was also appointed as an judge of the Madhya Pradesh High Court and Rajasthan High Court.

Hon. Justice Gyan Sudha Misra (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2010.

She Was also actively associated with the activities of the lawyers & the legal profession & hence was elected as a Treasurer, Joint Secretary, & Member Executive committee of the Supreme Court Bar Association, Several times, which is the premier Association of lawyers in the country.

Hon. Justice Asok Kumar Ganguly (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2008.

He also served as the Chairman of the West Bengal Human Rights Commission.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Madras High Court. He was also appointed as an judge of the Patna High Court and Calcutta High Court.

Hon. Justice Hemant Laxman Gokhale (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2009.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Madras High Court & Allahabad High Court.

He was appointed as Additional Judge of the Bombay High Court. He was also appointed as a permanent Judge of the Gujarat High Court.

Hon. Justice Sam Nariman Variava (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2000.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Delhi High Court.

He was appointed as Additional Judge of the Bombay High Court thereafter became permanent Judge on 12 June 1987.

Hon. Justice Bisheshwar Prasad Singh (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2001.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Bombay High Court.

He is the grandson of Bhuvaneshwar Prasad Sinha, 6th Chief Justice of India and his father Rameshwar Prasad Singh was a judge of the Patna High Court.

Hon. Justice Arijit Pasayat (Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2001.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Kerala High Court & Delhi High Court.

He was appointed as Additional Judge of the Orissa High Court.

Hon. Justice Swatanter Kumar(Ret.)

Appointed Former Judge of the Supreme court of India, 2009.

He is also Former Chairperson of the National Green Tribunal.

He has also been the Chief Justice of Bombay High Court. He was also appointed as an judge of the Delhi High Court and Punjab & Haryana High Court.

Hon. Justice Kshitij R. Vyas (Ret.)

Appointed Former Chief Justice of Bombay High Court, 2006.

He also worked as law representative of Uchha Nyayalaya Patrika, A law general of Central Government, A reporter of Gujarat Law Times & also was elected as Vice President of the Gujarat High Court Advocates Association for two terms.

Thereafter he was elevated to the bench of Gujarat High Court as additional Judge & later as President Judge.

Hon. Justice B.A. Khan (Ret.)

Appointed Former Chief Justice of Jammu & Kashmir High Court, 2007.

He has Served in various High Courts including being the Acting Chief Justice of the Delhi High Court. He has also worked as the president of SAARC Law India & as a member of the Executive Committee of the National Judicial Academy, Bhopal.

Furthermore, he has been the Chairman of the J & K State Accountability Commission.

He was also selected as the Chairman of the J & K State Accountability Commission (SAC)- an anti-graft body akin to the Lokpal in the year 2015.

Hon. Justice Vinod Kumar Gupta (Ret.)

Appointed Former First Chief Justice of Jharkhand High Court, 2000.

He has served as Advocate General of Jammu & Kashmir, Senior Central Government standing Counsel of Jammu & Kashmir High Court. Thereafter he joined as the Chief Justice of Himachal Pradesh High court in 2003. Before the retirement he also held the post of the Chief Justice of the Uttarakhand High court 2008.

Hon. Justice SN Jha (Ret.)

Appointed Former Chief Justice of Rajasthan High Court, 2005.

He joined bar in 15.07.1969 and was elevated as a judge of the Patna High Court in 1990. He was elevated as the 23rd Chief Justice of Jammu and Kashmir High court in 2004 and then appointed the Chief Justice of Rajasthan High Court in 2005, from where he retired on 15.06.2007.

Upon his retirement, Justice Jha was appointed as the President of Customs Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal. He resigned from the post in service of humanity and joined as the founder chairperson of the Bihar State Human Rights Commission from where he has retired in 2014.

He has been practicing in the Supreme Court of India since. He has been appointed as an arbitrator/ Court Commissioner in many cases.

Hon. Justice K K Lahoti (Ret.)

Appointed Former Acting Chief Justice of Madhya Pradesh, 2013.

He was also elected as the Additional Advocate General of Madhya Pradesh, at High Court Bench, Gwalior before being appointed ashs the Judge of High Court Of Madhya Pradesh. He was also the Executive Chairman, M.P. State Legal Services Authority and also Practiced Law on Constitutional, Civil & Taxation side from & Practiced on Civil & Criminal side at District Court Guna from. He also Conducted Mega Lok Adalat in Madhya Pradesh, in which a record number of matters were decided.

Hon. Justice Rakesh Tiwari (Ret.)

Appointed Senior Advocate , Supreme Court Of India, 2017-2021 & also conducting Arbitrations for some of the largest PSU’s of the country.

Appointed Acting Chief Justice, Calcutta High Court, 2002-2017.

He was also empaneled as a counsel of Central Government Undertaking Corporations, Banks, Local Bodies & Premier Companies before he got elevated as a Judge in 2002 & was in office till March, 2016. He retired as Acting Chief Justice of the Calcutta High Court in September 2017.
